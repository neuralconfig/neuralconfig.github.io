Why Gruvbox?

Gruvbox was designed by Pavel Pertsev with one goal: create a color scheme that's comfortable to work with for extended periods. The warm, earthy tones are easier on the eyes than bright neon colors, while still maintaining excellent contrast. The palette is inspired by retro groove aesthetics of the 1970s, giving it a unique, timeless quality. It's extremely popular among Vim users and terminal enthusiasts who value both functionality and aesthetics. This theme says you care about details and long-term comfort.
