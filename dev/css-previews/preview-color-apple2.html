Why Apple II Green?

The Apple II with its green phosphor monitor was revolutionary – it brought computing from universities and corporations into homes, schools, and small businesses. This color represents the democratization of technology. Unlike the pure black backgrounds of other terminals, Apple II monitors often showed a subtle dark green background due to the phosphor characteristics, creating a unique, softer aesthetic. This theme captures that pioneering spirit of accessible, personal computing.
