Why Commodore 64?

The C64 was a phenomenon – the best-selling single computer model of all time. Its distinctive blue screen and light blue PETSCII characters are instantly recognizable. This was the machine that democratized both computing and gaming, where the demo scene flourished, and where countless programmers got their start typing in BASIC programs from magazines. The color scheme is bold, unique, and represents the intersection of serious computing and fun – perfect for showing technical expertise with personality.
