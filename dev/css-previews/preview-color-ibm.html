Why IBM Green?

IBM green screen terminals were ubiquitous in corporate America throughout the 1980s and 1990s. This specific shade of green became synonymous with serious enterprise computing – banking systems, airline reservations, inventory management, and mission-critical business applications. For anyone who worked in IT during this era, this color instantly evokes the reliability and power of mainframe computing. Perfect for conveying enterprise-grade infrastructure expertise.
