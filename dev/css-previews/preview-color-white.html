Why White?

White phosphor monochrome terminals represented the purest form of early computing displays. Before color became standard, white-on-black offered maximum contrast and clarity. This aesthetic is timeless and professional, letting content speak for itself without the distraction of color. Perfect for a no-nonsense, focused presentation of technical information.
